About

Formerly was Quang Binh General Trading Company, was founded in accordance with the Decision No. 29/Q§-UB dated November 05, 1992 issued by People's Committee of Quang Binh province, on the basis absorption three companies: Quang Binh General Trading Company, Viet-Lao Commercial Company and the Domestic Trade Transportation Enterprise. And on April 16, 2004, the name of Quang Binh General Trading Company was changed by Quang Binh Industry and Trading Company following the Decision No. 1244/Q§-UB issued by People's Committee of Quang Binh province. In 2007, the Quang Binh Industry Trading Company converted to the joint stock company in accordance with the policies of Vietnam's Government and People's Committee of Quang Binh, changed name into Dai Truong Phat Mineral and Industry Joint Stock Corporation and officially came to operate from January 01, 2008.
