About

Formerly was Quang Binh General Trading Company, was founded in accordance with the Decision No. 29/Q§-UB dated November 05, 1992 issued by People's Committee of Quang Binh province, on the basis absorption three companies: Quang Binh General Trading Company, Viet-Lao Commercial Company and the Domestic Trade Transportation Enterprise. And on April 16, 2004, the name of Quang Binh General Trading Company was changed by Quang Binh Industry and Trading Company following the Decision No. 1244/Q§-UB issued by People's Committee of Quang Binh province. In 2007, the Quang Binh Industry Trading Company converted to the joint stock company in accordance with the policies of Vietnam's Government and People's Committee of Quang Binh, changed name into Dai Truong Phat Mineral and Industry Joint Stock Corporation and officially came to operate from January 01, 2008.

Juridical personality

Dai Truong Phat Mineral and Industry Joint Stock Corporation (business name: Dai Truong Phat Corporation) is established under the way of joint stock company the Enterprise Law adopted by the eleventh National Assembly of Socialist Republic of Vietnam.

Dai Truong Phat Corporation is established and operated the business activities in accordance with the registered business license No. 3100128390 issued by Quang Binh’s Department of Planning and Investment on December 15th, 2007 (seventh changes on October 03, 2010)

Dai Truong Phat Corporation has its private seal and independence on its assets. It is managed and controlled on the basis of Charter passed by stockholder's meeting.

Charter Capital: 160 Billions VND (One hundred and sixty billions Vietnam dong)

Legal representative:

Mr.Lê Văn Duẩn

Objectives

We strive to make Dai Truong Phat Corporation become one of the head enterprises in the filed of mineral exploitation and processing. We continue to promote the developing fields of industry such as:
  • Industry production of shaped aluminum, health care, construction
  • Production of plastic household products
  • Business in aluminum and discarded materials
  • Business general trade and bid construction

Business missions

  1. Dai Truong Phat Corporation builds and protects the stable investment values for the owners and partners based on the steady financial position, the professional management and manufacturing technologies.
  2. Dai Truong Phat Corporation associates harmonically the primary benefits of the investors, laborers and partners.
  3. Dai Truong Phat Corporation manages its staffs including the responsible, highly - qualified employees with the modern style of working in a professional environment.

Business structure:

  • Dai Truong Phat Corporation operates in accordance with the Charter passed by Members’ Council and the regulations of Laws.
  • Members’ Council is the most powerful staff of Dai Truong Phat Corporation.
  • Responsible to the Board for the operation of the Corporation as Chairman of the Board.

Subsidiary companies:

  • Global Mineral and Industry Co., Ltd.
  • Branch of Dai Truong Phat Mineral and Trade Enterprise.
  • Branch of Dai Truong Phat General Construction Enterprise.
  • Dai Truong Phat Plastics Joint Stock Company.
  • Sinh Quyen Joint Stock Company
  • Branch of Dai Truong Phat Transporting Enterprise.
  • Branch of Dong Hoi Trading and Service Center.
  • Branch of Guest House of Dai Truong Phat Corporation.
